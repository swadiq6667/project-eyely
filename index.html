Business Model

How We Operate

Our transaction flow is straightforward and transparent — from sourcing internationally verified products to delivering them to UAE consumers through regulated marketplace platforms.

01

Source from International Manufacturers

Products sourced directly from verified manufacturers in China, South Korea, Italy, and Taiwan with signed purchase agreements on file.

02

Import via UAE Customs Clearance

All goods imported through official UAE customs channels with complete documentation — invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin.

03

List on Amazon.ae & Noon.com

Products listed on verified seller accounts on UAE's leading e-commerce marketplaces, with full product descriptions and compliance labelling.

04

UAE Consumer Purchases Online

UAE consumers browse and purchase via Amazon.ae or Noon.com — both platforms manage payment collection and consumer protection.

05

Marketplace Revenue Settlement

Amazon.ae and Noon.com transfer collected revenue to the company's UAE bank account on a regular settlement cycle.

Transaction Flow at a Glance

  • Supplier Invoice → Purchase order confirmed with international manufacturer
  • UAE Import → Standard customs clearance, all documentation maintained
  • Marketplace Listing → Products active on Amazon.ae / Noon.com
  • Consumer Purchase → End customer buys via UAE marketplace
  • Revenue Settlement → Marketplace transfers to company UAE bank account
Platform Compliance

Amazon.ae and Noon.com conduct their own seller verification. Being an active seller on both platforms demonstrates our business legitimacy and operational readiness.
